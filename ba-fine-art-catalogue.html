This catalog was created for the final exhibition of works by graduates
of the BA (Hons) Fine Art 2022 at the British Higher School of Art &Design.
The title of the exhibition is 'clarify, expand, highlight'. All the graphic techniques, as well as the use of translucent paper, were aimed at emphasizing this conceptual underpinning of the exhibition.
