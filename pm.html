This catalog was created for the final exhibition of works by graduates of the
BA (Hons) Fine Art 2022 at the British Higher School of Art &Design. The title
of the exhibition is 'clarify, expand, highlight'. All the graphic techniques,
as well as the use of translucent paper,
were aimed at emphasizing this conce-
ptual underpinning of the exhibition.

This project combines graphic design
and illustration. The aim of the project was to create a design for a children's fairy tale. The layout, font selection and illustration style were based on Soviet children's books, after a thorough study of the visual features and conventions of the time.
